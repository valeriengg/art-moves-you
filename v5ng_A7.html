Art That Moves You

Artist @annalaura_art

Art is a true example of subjectiveness. An artist's interpretation of their own art can widely differ from their audience's. Recently, I came across the account @annalaura_art on tiktok. She specializes in comic strips and cartoonish animals, and her most recent posts feature short clips of a comic that tells sweet stories of her characters. Watching her tiktoks made me realize how art can truly influence or motivate their audience. While her creations affected me strongly, they might be something completely bland to another viewer.

Different Art Mediums

Just from taking a glance at her profiles, you can tell her favourite medium of art is watercolour. However, that is not the only form of art she likes to create with. Here is a list of the different medias of art Anna-Laura uses:

  1. Watercolour
  2. Digital art
  3. Marker ink
  4. Oil pastels
  5. Coffee spills
  6. Pencil sketch

It's so interesting that she can create art with so many different mediums, especially coffee stains! In this tiktok, she pours coffee and draws defining lines with her blank ink marker throughout the spill to create a beautiful piece. Her art resonates with many followers, and in a post on Instagram, she mentions in the caption to be patient as you are still growing. This can be applied to many situations but with the context of the post, it seems she is encouraging followers to be patient with their art or any struggles they currently experience.

Hopefully, you were able to find some tiktoks to connect with and share the warmth with other viewers. My favourite tiktok of hers wasn't linked within this post so maybe we'll have the same favourite! 😉
